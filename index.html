Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Overview

DevFolio ("we", "us", or "our") operates as a browser-based portfolio builder. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service at devfolio.app.

2. Data We Collect

DevFolio operates entirely in your browser. All portfolio data you enter — including your name, bio, skills, projects, and uploaded images — is processed and stored locally in your browser session only. We do not transmit this data to any server.

  • Local Processing: All data stays on your device
  • No Accounts: We don't require registration
  • No Cookies: We don't use tracking cookies
  • No Analytics: We don't collect usage statistics

3. Uploaded Images

Profile images and project images you upload are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript's FileReader API. Images are converted to base64 and embedded in the generated HTML file. They are never uploaded to our servers.

4. Exported Files

When you download your portfolio (HTML or ZIP), the file is generated entirely client-side. The download happens directly from your browser without passing through our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to load typography. When the page loads, your browser may make requests to fonts.googleapis.com. Google's own privacy policy governs this interaction.

6. Children's Privacy

DevFolio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
